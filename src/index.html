    Modes

    Preset Modes

    Breakaway from the age old yellow and white light and surround yourself with lighting according to mood. Through its preset configurations one can customise different modes - reading mode, relaxation mode, Party mode etc. You can define your party mode and have Lite cycle through different colors at random or use the blues greens and purples to define your relaxation mode.

    Spam Free Messaging

    Spam Free Messaging

    Lite’s unique spam free messaging feature avoids alerts from unwanted numbers and contacts.

    Music Sync

    Music Sync

    Elevate your music experience with the Lite’s music sync feature. Lite changes its color and syncs with the rhythm of the music. Lite syncs with the music playing from your smart phone and syncs in beautifully to provide a live Light & Sound show.

    Weather Alert

    Weather Alert

    Experience IOTA Lite to better visualize the weather so you’d know whether you need an umbrella, a coat or a sunglasses. IOTA Lite visually shows what weather forecast has in store for you at your location. App offers a simple customization option though which you can choose any shade to denote different weather conditions.

    Call/SMS Alert

    Call/SMS alert

    Never miss an important call as Lite with its unique call alert feature will blink in a certain color if you’ve got a call coming in, which is great when you have your loud music playing in or have your phone in a silent mode. You can customize your app with absolute ease and can only allow few selected contacts to alert you via Lite
